# Strain Relief 
<img src="Preview%20-%20Strain%20Relief%20-%20Adafruit%2020v%20USBC%2090%20Degree.jpg" alt="drawing" width="50%"/>

Some form of strain relief is recommended; having the control board closer to the motor raises the potential stress on the power input.

*See STEP subdirectory for a template for bolt patterns*

## Assembly Advice
Even if you do not have a strain relief designed or that fits your need, consider inserting the nuts and two screw during assembly to make adding your strain relief easier later.
